My current research focus is on traffic modelling and future state maximisation. Our working group developed a traffic model that outperforms others in terms of computing time.

Methodologically I am focused on networks analysis and microscopical modelling approaches in the realm of Monte Carlo and Agent-based simulation.

To keep up to date with my research, follow me on ResearchGate. In my research profile all relevant activities are listed.
